Recipe_FinderApp
├── Creates NotificationMgr + NotificationDelegate
├── Injects notificationMgr into environment
└── ContentView
    ├── Creates PantryManager
    ├── Injects pantryManager into SearchRecipeView + ShoppingCartView
    │
    ├── SearchRecipeView (Tab 1)
    │   ├── Owns RecipeAPI
    │   ├── Reads @AppStorage preferences
    │   └── Navigates to RecipeDetailView (row tap)
    │       ├── Uses pantryManager (highlight pantry items)
    │       ├── Uses notificationMgr (schedule reminders)
    │       ├── Fetches ingredients from API
    │       └── Reads @AppStorage (showNutrition)
    │
    ├── ShoppingCartView (Tab 2)
    │   └── Uses pantryManager (add/remove items)
    │
    ├── AppInfoView (Tab 3)
    │   └── Reads Bundle.main (name, version, build, copyright)
    │
    └── SettingsView (Tab 4)
        └── Reads/Writes @AppStorage preferences
